Common procedure
1. Confirmation

Confirmation of your request

2. Assets review

I will review your character design, your idea for the request and, accessories (if any, regarding your idea for the request) and see if I can work with it

3. sketchie

I will approximate your idea in a quick rough sketch wireframes and see if you like it

4. Payment

If settled, I will send you the invoice and you can start payment

5. Packaging

Once done, I will notify you and pack the finished commission into a zip file. The file includes the project file (Clip or PSD) and the raw export
